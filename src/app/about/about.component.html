 The Mission

We strive to aide in the skills and development of children in surrounding cities and counties. Since 2015, we've been dedicated to training with children, ages five to nineteen, in areas involving ball handling, shooting form, speed, and coordination; while incorporating life lessons along the way.

The Owner Tirrell Cumberland

Tirrell is the owner and head trainer at TC3. Before starting his business, he had his own success in basketball where he toured with the Harlem Globetrotters in 2013. In his college career, he played two season at Ohio Christian University, scoring 1,275 points and was a two-time NCCAA All American Player. Previous to playing at OCU, Cumberland played two seasons at Southern State Community College, scoring 1,583 points and was an All American player there, as well. Tirrell graduated from Hillsboro High School and scored 1,150 in three seasons.
